About Me

I discovered yoga as a teenager, and what began as curiosity became a lifelong calling. In 2015 I began my formal study in the Vedic sciences, and over the past decade I have taught in private, studio, retreat, and virtual settings across the world — bringing an integrative approach that weaves together all three pillars of the Vedic traditions.

My path has been shaped as much by life as by study. As a 2x cancer survivor and serial entrepreneur, I have navigated profound personal transformation alongside the demands of building and leading new businesses and organizations. These experiences inform the way I show up for every client, with both learned wisdom and lived understanding.

The Vedic Arts & Sciences

I am a long-time student of Dr. Justine Lemos, PhD. Our lineage integrates three ancient modalities that work together as a complete system:

  • Yoga (Yoke / Union) for the Mind: Certified YTT-500 yoga instructor via Western Yoga College. I teach postural yoga, meditation, subtle body anatomy (chakras), pranayama, philosophy, and more.
  • Ayurveda (The Science of Life) for the Body: Certified Ayurvedic Lifestyle Counselor offering comprehensive health and lifestyle assessments with personalized practices and remedies.
  • Jyotish (The Science of Light) for the Spirit: Trained, intuitive Vedic astrologer using birth charts to provide spiritual counseling and pragmatic advice and insights for life and business.

Technology & Entrepreneurship

I've spent my career building businesses from the ground up, working for nearly 20 years alongside founders, leaders, and innovators. Today I advise early-stage tech companies on strategy and growth, drawing on hands-on experience as a startup co-founder and CEO.
